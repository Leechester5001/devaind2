About Us

Manufacturer of Precision Sheet metal Press Components & Sub Assemblies.

  • Deva Industries is a reputable manufacturer of precision sheet metal press components and sub-assemblies. The company was founded in 1992 and is located in the Peenya Industrial Area in Bangalore, India. At Deva Industries, we pride ourselves on our ability to deliver quality products and services to our customers.
  • We continuously analyze risks and opportunities to improve our processes and innovate, ensuring that we maintain a safe and clean environment for our employees. We provide regular training and encourage the participation of our employees to ensure that we continually review the effectiveness of our quality management systems. Our dedication to quality has enabled us to establish ourselves as a leading manufacturer in the sheet metal press components and sub-assemblies industry.
  • We have a team of experienced professionals who are passionate about delivering the best results to our customers. Deva Industries has a strong reputation for providing quality products and services. We take pride in our ability to meet our customers' needs and exceed their expectations. If you're looking for a reliable manufacturer of precision sheet metal press components and sub-assemblies, look no further than Deva Industries.
